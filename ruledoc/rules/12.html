Rationale

AcceptEventAction is 'an action that waits for the occurrence of an event meeting a specified condition'. The Action has a Trigger (the event) and a Result (an output pin that holds the accepted event).

This variation is applied as step 1 of the second limb of the exclusive dependency. It deals with the special case in which the source and target of the transition are the same state. The activeState remains the same, but a new behaviorExecution node is created.
